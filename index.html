Project (1/6) - Fast Totem
Fast Totem is a Minecraft: Bedrock edition addon that will allow you to swap your undying totem and shield between your hands by only clicking them. Swapping will preserve all item data, and also adds a totem first activation and first use (achievement) alert, triggered when a player uses a totem! This alerts is completely customizable
